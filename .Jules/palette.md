# Palette's UX Journal

## 2025-12-14 - Missing Tooltips on Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** The application heavily relies on `IconButton` and `FloatingActionButton` without semantic labels or tooltips. This makes the interface inaccessible to screen reader users and confusing for mouse users who rely on hover states.
**Action:** Systematically add `tooltip` properties to all icon-only buttons during development.
